Favorite Brainrot Song Compilation

When it comes to brainrot songs. I always go to SecretAsian6's channel as he is a master. His vocals for these songs are amazing and the way he incorporates brainrot terms/lingo into popular mainstream songs is beautiful. Heres a compilation of my favorites.

"The Less I Know The Better" by Tame Impala but It's Brainrot(The Less I Goon The Better)

Amazing beat and a classic brainrot parody of the amazing original song.

"Sparks" by Coldplay but it's Brainrot(Barks)

This is also one of my favorites. Just like the original Sparks by Coldplay, it dwells on emotion and you can simply just feel it...

"BIRDS OF A FEATHER" by Billie Eilish but it's Brainrot

This is my absolute favorite brainrot song. Theres so much emotion and soul put into the song it makes me feel alive. The original is "Birds of a Feather" by Billie Eilish which is also a banger.
