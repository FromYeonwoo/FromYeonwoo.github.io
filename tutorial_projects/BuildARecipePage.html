Fried Egg Sandwich

A simple recipe that consists of a fried egg sandwiched between two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • Oil of Choice
  • Bread of Choice
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1-2 Extra Large Eggs

Instructions

  1. Heat up oil in pan
  2. Crack egg(s) and add it to frying pan
  3. Sprinkle the salt and pepper onto the egg
  4. Flip the egg halfway through to get both sides crispy
  5. Insert egg between two slices of bread slices of choice
  6. Enjoy!
